Future blog for previous website.

Well, actually, it was suposed to be a blog for Gutovska & Partners Law Firm website, as it was, the blog-link in site-navigation lead to an old version. But curently its future is unclear. It has been pending for client's confirmation on various points for a long time now.

Chronologically this wasn't the next project after the website for the law firm. Before that I worked some with Wordpress, including one of the projects farther on the list. Wordpress is considered "blog-oriented" CMS so to speak. So after making its acquaintance I decided to try to use it for what it was created. It was decided then to develop new (more modern and interesting-looking) version of the existing blog.

Actually, there isn't much to tell about the development process, since it was supposed to be just a blog and nothing else. It pretty much consisted of choosing a theme, editing it a little, adding some styling to it, and configuring a few plugins (such as custom fields, infinite scroll and subscription form for example).
