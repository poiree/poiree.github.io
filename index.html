About Us

Gutovska & Partners offers state of the art legal advice and support of business communities in the most innovative industries of IT and Outsourcing, Agriculture, Energy and Renewables, Media and Visual Art. We have gathered in one team the most gifted legal experts who will guide your business across the field of complex rules and regulations both on domestic and international levels. Our lawyers are dedicated to providing exclusively professional legal services with precise attention to every case.

  • AGRIBUSINESS

    Gutovska & Partners consults Ukrainian and foreign agriculture business on land acquisition operations, assists with registration procedures as well as obtaining certificates and licenses. We give professional advise on legal aspects of employment of seasonal workers, insurance, tax, conflicts with State agriculture inspection. Agriculture Team of Gutovska & Partners does everything possible to secure the most comfortable conditions for your agribusiness in Ukraine.

  • ENERGY AND RENEWABLES

    Energy & Renewables team of Gutovska & Partners helps your business obtain the most favorable treatment from government and local authorities. We provide legal services on patenting and licensing, innovation technologies, customer relations, as well as help to resolve internal and corporate issues regarding intellectual property rights. We have a separate team of taxation experts to answer all the questions regarding VAT refund, tax preferences and tax regimes. Also we understand that the large percentage of Energy & Renewables market functions on international level. Ukrainian and EU companies are actively conducting various kinds of commercial transactions - we provide top-level legal support of such operations.

  • INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES

    Gutovska & Partners is proud to be number one representative of Ukrainian IT companies in domestic, international and arbitration courts. Our professional negotiators are ready to take part in negotiations on your behalf to secure the best deal you can possibly get. IT Industry Team of Gutovska & Partners consults on day-to-day business activities, corporate issues, contracts drafting, patents and innovation registering, services and products export, databases registering, labor issues and tax considering peculiarities of your business.

  • MEDIA AND ART

    Gutovska & Partners offers top-tier legal services in the area of intellectual property, advertising, visual and musical art, TV and radio, art projects, media campaigns, literature and mass culture events. Our Intellectual property team has in-depth knowledge and experience in drafting investment projects and startup agreements. Our lawyers represent clients in governmental and local authorities, including the State service of intellectual property, State regulatory service, Antimonopoly Committee, national and foreign courts. We provide legal consultation on a wide range of employment, tax, insurance, contracts drafting, negotiation and mediation issue and procedures considering unique features of your media business.

  • Corporate Governance

    Corporate governance practice at Gutovska & Partners includes legal consultation of board members, senior management, and board committees on their scope of fiduciary duties and a wide range of corporate governance matters. Our corporate law consultants are always ready to help with any legal issues that may arise in the corporate field.

  • Intellectual property and IT

    Our IP professionals help clients cooperate with government authorities to comply with applicable intellectual property laws and regulations, including patent, copyright and trademark registration. Our mediation and litigation experts are able to resolve any dispute related to trademark, license, copyright, trade secrets or patent infringements. We are proud to be one the leading experts among Ukrainian law firms in international transactions on transfer of intellectual property among worldwide business communities.

  • Litigation and Arbitration

    Our Litigation and Arbitration team comprises of experienced attorneys trained in dispute resolution both in state courts (domestic and foreign) and arbitration tribunals around the world. They will help you on all stages of dispute resolution: from drafting claims and complaints, to participating in trials, judicial and arbitration proceedings.

  • Mediation

    Mediation is widely used as one of the most effective alternative means of dispute resolution. Experienced negotiator can act as a third party in dispute and can help the parties resolve any legal or business dispute, without resorting to lengthy and costly arbitration or litigation procedures. You can use the help of our skilled negotiators if you want to resolve any controversy with your partners outside of the court room.

  • Mergers and Acquisitions

    As part of our legal practice, we help corporations conduct all operations and transactions in the course of mergers and acquisitions. Out of multiple aspects of this complicated practice, we specialize in: Restructuring transactions within the company; Asset management transactions; Dispositions and related financing arrangements; Entity selection and formation; Operating and partnership agreements.

  • Labor and Employment Law

    Gutovska & Partners acted as a representative for employers and employees in a wide spectrum of labor and employment cases. We consult foreign businesses who operate in Ukraine regarding peculiarities of Ukrainian labor law, including rights and obligations of employers and employees. In addition, we advise clients on compliance issues under various labor and employment laws and regulations.

  • Agricultural law

    Laws and regulations cover many aspects of agriculture: land use, the rights of farmers, the use of seeds, pesticides and fertilizers, environmental issues and many more. Innovative processes and technologies streamline the development of agriculture. Inventions in this sphere raise many issues related to intellectual property, patents, trademarks and trade secrets. The evolution of agriculture also implies issues of biotechnology, hydrology and engineering. That is why agriculture business is need of professional legal experts who have skills and knowledge to help the industry players in their everyday operation.

  • Taxes

    Gutovska & Partners is knowledgeable about a broad range of domestic and international tax related matters, on both the contentious and non-contentious side. On the regular basis, we conduct tax advisory work on behalf of our clients who work in various innovative industries: from agriculture to IT. On the contentious side, we represent our corporate clients in disputes with public tax authorities.

  • Anti-trust/Competition

    Competition laws and rules constantly keep evolving in all jurisdictions around the globe. Legal environment of competition is highly-regulated and it poses tangible liability risks for business communities. Gutovska & Partners possess insight and expertise of local competition laws and practices. Whether it is a growing business or a multinational corporation, we make sure that our clients are aware of all competition rules and standards relevant to their business.

  • Private clients

    Gutovska & Partners is the exclusive provider of legal counsel to high net worth individuals and wealthy families. Our advice helps derive a greater value from companies or assets they own or control. We are able to handle various legal and contractual aspects of key life events: drafting marriage agreements, resolving disputes between the members of families, administering wills and trusts. Our experience in the field allows us to handle complex, multi-jurisdictional estate and tax disputes related to family assets and wealth.
